Who am I?

Hello, my name is Cori Tanck. I was born in Maine, moved to Florida where I lived the most of my life. I moved to Chicago 4 years ago and have dove head first into the interior design world. I am currently working for Frank Ponterio Interior Design and I have been learning so much. My goal is to continue to grow within the design industry and learn as much as possible along the way. I have met many great individuals and I cant wait to see where else this wonderful journey takes me.

I am very much interested in freelance work in my spare time and welcome any opportunity to create a space which you will enjoy to live in. Please email me your personal info and the scope of work you are interested in completing, along with any pitures of the space or spaces.
